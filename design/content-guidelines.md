# Content Guidelines

## Primary focus

- Present Tech Mountain as the dependable IT department for small and medium businesses.
- Highlight managed IT, security, system stability, and support.
- Keep web design and search marketing mentions secondary.

## Headline style

- Use direct, reassuring language.
- Emphasize calm expertise and business continuity.
- Keep sentences simple and benefit-focused.

## Copy approach

- Use short paragraphs and bold clarity.
- Avoid overly technical terms unless they explain value.
- Speak as a partner: we protect your operations, not just fix problems.

## Editing the starter

- Update text in `src/data/content.js` for messaging changes.
- Add new sections by creating components in `src/components/`.
- Add new pages later under `src/pages/` and route them through `App.jsx`.
