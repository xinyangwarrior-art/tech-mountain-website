# Brand Voice

## Tone

- Trusted, steady, and highly competent.
- Professional, calm, and reassuring.
- Approachable, plainspoken, and human.
- "We just make it work so you can focus on your business."

## Messaging focus

- Tech Mountain is the IT department for your business.
- We handle technology so owners and teams can stay productive.
- We speak clearly, respect your time, and keep systems secure.
- We build confidence through experience, reliability, and consistent support.

## Voice principles

- Use clear, direct language.
- Avoid jargon unless it helps explain value.
- Reinforce that customers are in control while Tech Mountain is managing IT.
- Position services as dependable and easier than doing it alone.
