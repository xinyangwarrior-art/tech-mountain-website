# Tech Mountain Website RPD

## Project goal

Create a polished, modern website starter that positions Tech Mountain as the trusted IT department for small and medium businesses.

## Core outcomes

- Lead generation through clear, direct contact calls to action.
- Messaging that centers IT support, managed services, and reliability.
- A calm, professional brand voice that feels reassuring and competent.
- A structure that is easy to expand, update, and maintain via GitHub.

## Audience

- Owners and managers of small-to-medium businesses.
- Decision makers who need dependable IT support and want to outsource their technology burden.
- Customers who value stability, plainspoken advice, and a partner who can "just make it work."

## Design principles

- Clear hierarchy: support messaging first, web and marketing services secondary.
- Clean spacing and typography for professional readability.
- Blue-white palette with black and neutral accents for authority.
- Strong trust elements: service summary, local contact details, experience, and reassurance.

## Recommended site sections

1. Hero section with value statement and contact CTA.
2. Core services overview: Managed IT, Security & Monitoring, Cloud and Hosting.
3. Why Tech Mountain: dependable team, calm support, and local business focus.
4. Featured outcomes: support as your IT department, fewer interruptions, simpler technology.
5. Contact and local details with a direct phone CTA and request form placeholder.

## Template approach

- Use React for structure and easier future scaling.
- Store primary messaging in `src/data/content.js` so copy changes are isolated.
- Keep page sections as components for future page expansion.
- Use static assets and SVGs to maintain fast loading without heavy imagery.

## Future work

- Add service detail pages and routing.
- Add a CMS, markdown content layer, or GitHub-driven content folder.
- Add testimonials, case studies, and updated local trust signals.
